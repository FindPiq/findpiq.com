A Parent's Mission

When our family moved to Switzerland, my daughter faced a challenge every expat child knows — new languages, new words, a whole new world to decode. I watched her spend time on screens, and I kept thinking: what if this time could actually teach her something?

As a parent, I became obsessed with making screen time meaningful. I wanted my daughter to learn vocabulary across languages — not through boring flashcards, but through the joy of discovery, stories, and play.

So I built FindPiq.

Every puzzle, every story, every "Did You Know?" card was designed with one question in mind: would my own child love this, and would she actually learn from it? The answer had to be yes to both.

Today, FindPiq is made by a small, passionate team based in Switzerland. We pour our hearts into every detail because we're not just building an app — we're building the learning tool we wished existed for our own kids.
